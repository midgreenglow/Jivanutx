Your Personalized Gut Health Report

Your gut is home to trillions of microbes that influence digestion, immunity, metabolism, and overall well-being. Jivanu Atlas™ offers a science-driven way to explore this hidden ecosystem-giving you a deeper understanding of your gut microbiome through advanced sequencing and expert interpretation.

A Simple Way to Know Your Gut

Using our easy-to-use at-home collection kit, you provide a small stool (poop) sample. The sample is analyzed using long-read 16S rRNA sequencing, enabling deep and accurate profiling of your gut microbiome.

The result is a personalized gut health report that translates complex microbiome data into clear, meaningful insights-for individuals and clinicians alike.

What’s Inside Your Jivanu Atlas™ Report

Gut Richness Score

A measure of microbial diversity-an important indicator of gut resilience.

Gut Inflammation Score

A microbiome-informed score reflecting patterns associated with gut inflammation.

Pathogen Load Assessment

Screening for potentially harmful or opportunistic microbes.
