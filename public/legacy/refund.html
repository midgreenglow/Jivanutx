Refund Policy

Last Updated: 03 February 2026

At Jivanu Therapeutics Pvt. Ltd., we are committed to maintaining the highest standards of service across our microbiome-based offerings. Due to the customized, biological, and analytical nature of our services, refund eligibility is assessed carefully and in accordance with regulatory, operational, and scientific considerations.

For billing-related inquiries or to submit a refund request, please contact our support team via the Contact Us page with relevant order details.
