NovaBiome™

Next-Generation Microbiome Therapeutics Platform

Novabiome™ is Jivanu Therapeutics’ innovative platform for precision microbial therapies, designed to go beyond conventional FMT. Our approach focuses on safety, specificity, and disease-targeted microbiome restoration. Currently in pre-clinical stages.

Partner With Us

Consortia Development

Pure, fully screened bacterial cultures—pathogen-free, safe, and ready to restore gut health. We select specific strains based on their functional roles and symbiotic relationships within the gut ecosystem to create highly effective, stable microbial communities.

Engineered Therapeutics

Smart probiotics carrying disease-targeted payloads for complex gut disorders, metabolic conditions, and cancer. These next-generation microbes are activated only within specific environments, ensuring high efficacy with no systemic toxicity.

Why NovaBiome™ Matters

Safe, precise, and next-generation microbial therapy-scientifically guided for real-world impact. We are moving beyond general restoration to targeted interventions that address the root cause of complex diseases.
