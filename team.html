The Jivanu Team

Our team comprises a multidisciplinary group of scientists, clinicians, and innovators dedicated to unraveling the complexities of the microbiome. With expertise spanning bioinformatics, microbiology, and clinical research, we are united in our mission to transform patient care through precision microbiome therapeutics.

Our Mission

To empower people with deep biological insights that lead to better health outcomes. We are dedicated to translating complex microbiome data into precision-driven therapies that target the root cause of disease.

Our Vision

A world where microbiome health is as foundational to medicine as genetics. We envision a future where personalized microbial interventions are accessible to all.
